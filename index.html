🎯 Strategic Overview

The intersection of DORA and eIDAS 2.0 creates both regulatory burden and strategic opportunity. This dashboard guides insurers through six compliance domains and traces each control back to its underlying eIDAS and DORA articles.

✅ Validation against eIDAS_text_analysis.pdf — corrections applied

  • Art. 19a added — 24-hour QTSP breach notification SLA is correctly mapped to Art. 19a (not Art. 19).
  • Art. 39 corrected — Art. 39 refers to qualified electronic time stamps (QTS). HSM seal-creation keys are now correctly mapped to Art. 29 / 29a (QSCD & QTSP key management).
  • Art. 46a added — QERDS (Qualified Electronic Registered Delivery Services) is correctly under Art. 46a, not 43/44 (which cover seals).
  • Art. 46b refined — now correctly cited only for electronic-document non-discrimination, not QTSP conformity (which is Art. 20/24).
  • Topic labels added — every article reference now shows its official topic (e.g., "Wallets", "Trust lists", "QWACs") so reviewers can verify mappings at a glance.
  • All other article references in the previous version were confirmed correct against the PDF.

How to use this dashboard

  • ✅ Tick off compliance tasks — progress auto-saves in your browser.
  • 👤 Assign an owner, due date and priority for each task.
  • 🔗 Open the Traceability Matrix to see control ↔ eIDAS ↔ DORA mappings with topic labels.
  • 📤 Export progress as JSON or a traceability CSV.
